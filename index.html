Child Safety

At Qweetly Studios, we take child safety seriously. Here are some resources and tips to help keep your child safe while playing our games:

  • Monitor your child's playtime and ensure they take regular breaks.
  • Encourage your child to play games that are age-appropriate.
  • Teach your child about online safety and the importance of not sharing personal information.
  • Use parental controls to manage your child's gaming experience.

If you have any concerns or questions about child safety, please don't hesitate to contact us.
